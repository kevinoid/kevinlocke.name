Welcome!

My name is Kevin Locke. I'm an independent software engineer and consultant from Bozeman, Montana. I'm a generalist who enjoys working on a broad range of technologies, in many roles, and across many disciplines. I'm also a proponent of free and open-source software, and a contributor to many projects. I aspire to promote social good in my work and personally by being supportive, compassionate, and empathetic to the best of my ability. When I'm not at the computer, you can find me hiking, biking, and skiing in the Montana mountains.

Professional Background

A few highlights from my professional background:

  • Graduated magna cum laude with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the Cornell University College of Engineering in Ithaca, NY in 2007.
  • Co-founded the software development company Digital Engine Software in Bozeman, MT with Mike Archer in 2009, recruiting Peter Nix soon after.
  • Joined early-stage fintech startup Quantpost in Missoula, MT in 2013, eventually serving as Director of Engineering.
  • Began serving diverse clients as an independent software engineer and consultant in Bozeman, MT in 2016, which I continue to find rewarding to this day.
